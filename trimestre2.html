Writing 1 corrected


Remote learning


We live in a world in which the remote learning is litle by litle becoming more common in the society.Since the circumstances that we are going through and the advancement of technology encourages to use more.


One of the main advantages is that help the student and the teacher to teach the class without having to leave their own home. Another advantage is that the student with the teacher's permission can record the class and watch it later.


Also has some negative aspect, such as that if the classes are not well prepared to take place like this, by the school, it may not be heard well, the connection is bad, it does not look good, etc.


From my point of view the government should promote these types of class, since this is a very good option for the times we are in, and because it is a great evolution for the future.


  1. SINCE?? – I do not know what you want to transmit with SINCE.
  2. …that IT helps…
  3. Use another connector not to repeat `advantage´ again – What is more,… / Moreover
  4. …It has…
  5. Omit `THAT´
  6. to take place like this, by the school (OMIT IT)
  7. this type of clases

The writing is well distributed into paragraphs but maybe you must use a wider range of connectors to make it more attractive. Apart from that, be careful with the mistakes you made.

Economic development: A solution or cause of poverty?


Currently it is claimed that we live in a world in which very few people have a lot of money and many people do not have enough, however there are both arguments for and against it


One of the main advantages of economic development is that it lets us live better.


Another advantage is that it makes more workplaces for everyone


On the opposite side, although there are many developed countries in the world, there are still a lot of poor countries.


Taking everything into account, from my point of view, the economic development is not a cause of poverty at all, but a solution. Since, as we see in developed countries, it is the way for the well-being of the society.


  1. enough, however – Better using a full—stop.

  3. Less than 120 WORDS.

Also, different tenses must be used, not just the present simple one.

Guillermo Future


First of all, I can see myself from a professional point of view, fifteen years from now, working in my own company, out of Linares. Also with some personal work like selling my own drawings, or training people in calisthenics.


I think that I will live in my own house, in a city with a beach, since I would like to study a degree in naval engineering.


Secondly, from the family point of view, I hope to continue meeting all my family, and who knows, maybe I know my life partner. As well I would love to have a pet.


Finally, I expect to travel to different parts of the world, to visit and test, every bar park that I can, and to know new places and many people.
